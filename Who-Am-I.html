Who Am I?

My name is Ian Holzman, I grew up in Seattle, WA, and moved to Vancouver, BC after graduating high school where I'm currently studying electrical engineering.  Outside of engineering, the things that I love to do the most are traveling, skiing, hiking and playing ultimate frisbee. I've had the chance to live in Denmark for eight months, hike and ski some of the best mountains in the pacific northwest, and score the game winning goal to send UBC men's ultimate to their first nationals tournament in eight years. To the right of this paragraph I have included pictures from those and some of my other favorite moments of my life.
